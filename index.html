Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the food waste estimation?

The food waste estimation is based on general averages for each food type. The results may vary depending on various factors, such as storage conditions and local practices.

Can I use the tool for all types of food?

Yes! The calculator supports various food types such as vegetables, fruits, meats, dairy, and grains. We continually update the tool to include more food types in the future.

How can I reduce food waste at home?

Our calculator provides tips specific to each food type. You can also minimize food waste by planning meals in advance, storing food properly, and utilizing leftovers creatively.
